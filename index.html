Van Voyage

Every van holds a story

Van voyage is a yearly event located in Europe where all campervan users gather to connect with each other. This one week will be jam-packed with activities catered to our lovely community. Think of Huishoudbeurs or vakantiebeurs, but for campervans lovers!
