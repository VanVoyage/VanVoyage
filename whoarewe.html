Meet the Team

Keverne Arthur — Head of Content

Keverne Arthur is a 26 year old toddler in terms of travel. He took his first baby steps at the age of 24 by leaving the Caribbean for the first time to travel to the Netherlands to further his education. This surreal transition from living on an island to living in a big city was a mind blowing experience that forced him to realize how important travelling actually is. Although he was extremely late to the travel scene he felt like it still worked out for the best. At this age he can 100% appreciate the little things like ridiing on a train compared to someone who grew up with trains their whole life.

D'ryce Bessems — Head of Management

D'ryce is the Head of Management of this project! His passion for traveling started at a young age. He loves going to different countries with his family and friends and loves experiencing different cultures. His go-to goal when traveling is making new friends across—the—world!

Jamie Bron —Head of Production

Jamie's parents are actually fellow campervan lovers and introduced her to travelling at a very young age in the style they prefer the most, campervan style! This early exposure allowed for her deep bond for campervan trips to take root in her soul and will live on forever.

Chantoni  Da Cunha — Head of Marketing

Chantoni has travelled to many places around the world from a very young age, but recently found a love for traveling by camper vans. She loves journaling and making core memories with the people that she has travelled with. She has spent most of her life traveling with her mom, but now that she is 22 years old she would like to now travel with her new friends that she recently made. She wants to get back into the traveling scene very soon, and visit a new continent like Africa one day!
