Class 2 Group 4





Problem


Top 3 problems
  • People sometimes feel lonely on the road
  • People don’t have someone to travel with
  • People don’t know like-minded people who are in the same situation as they are.
  • While on the road people have no way to find a traveling buddy.

Solution


Top 3 features
  • Creating an event that is organized yearly that is each time located somewhere else in Europe.
  • An event where people can exchange information and connect with each other including some workshops and competitions.
  • Have a forum on our website where people outside of our event can connect with and meet during their trips.

Unique Value Proposition


Single, clear and compelling message that states why you are different and worth buying

Vanvoyage is an exciting community event where camper van enthusiasts of all ages come together to learn, compete, and most importantly make lifelong connections. With workshops, competitions, and family-friendly activities, Vanvoyage offers an impactful experience that celebrates the spirit of traveling and adventure.

Unfair Advantage


Can’t be easily copied or bought
  • A community feeling like none other.
  • Opportunities regarding partnerships.
